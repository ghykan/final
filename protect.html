WHY CHICKEN
Chicken is the go-to protein source for athletes and bodybuilders. But beef also contains a high level of protein and a significant amount of creatine, which helps produce lean muscle much more quickly. The level of protein found in both protein and beef varies, but the differences are minor. However, your body can only absorb 74% of a slab of beef, where as your body can take in 80% of protein in chicken.
